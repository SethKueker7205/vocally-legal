Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Vocally ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you install and use the Vocally app on the Shopify App Store.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Vocally, we access and store the following information:

  • Shopify store data: Product titles, descriptions, vendor, product type, and tags — accessed through the Shopify Admin API to generate product descriptions.
  • Brand Voice settings: The tone, vocabulary, selling points, and example copy you enter in the Brand Voice setup form. This is stored so Vocally can apply your voice consistently.
  • Subscription information: Your plan status and usage count, managed through Shopify's billing system.

We do not collect personal information from your customers or store visitors. We do not collect payment card information.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide the Vocally service:

  • Product data and Brand Voice settings are sent to Anthropic's API to generate product descriptions. Anthropic processes this data as a sub-processor to deliver the service.
  • Brand Voice settings are stored in our database to apply your voice consistently across all generated descriptions.
  • Usage counts are tracked to enforce plan limits on the Basic plan.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Vocally uses Anthropic (claude.ai) to generate product descriptions. When you generate a description, the relevant product information and your saved Brand Voice settings are sent to Anthropic's API. Anthropic's use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy.

Vocally is built on and distributed through Shopify. Shopify's handling of your data is governed by their Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your Brand Voice settings and usage data are stored securely in our database. We use industry-standard practices to protect this data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. We do not store product content after a description has been generated and returned to you.

5. Data Retention

Your Brand Voice settings and subscription data are retained for as long as your store has Vocally installed. When you uninstall Vocally, your data is deleted within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. You can also delete your Brand Voice settings directly within the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Vocally is a business tool intended for Shopify merchants. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of Vocally after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
